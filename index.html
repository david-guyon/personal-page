ENU Taxis

During my Bachelor, I had a "Third Year Group Project". My group and I chose to make a booking system for taxis for students at Napier university. We made a website and an Android application in which you can set up a journey and book for a taxis for a specific date/time. The user is also able to see the booking he/she made and to remove them if needed. You can look at the website we made by clicking on the link below.
