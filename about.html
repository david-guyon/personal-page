About David Guyon

My name is David Guyon, I am 22 years old and I’m still student. After I obtained my University’s degree in Electronic and Computer Science in Angers, I went for 9 months in Scotland to continue my studies. I did a Bachelor of Engineering in Computing at Edinburgh Napier University. I obtained my Bachelor's degree and now I do a Master’s degree in Computer Science at the ISTIC laboratory in Rennes (France).

I decided to study IT because I’m passionate by electronic and programming.

By electronic, I mean Arduino cards, the Raspberry Pi with its Gertboard I/O extension and also electronics in general, either analog and/or digital. According to my motivation, I’ll post tutorials about electronics on my blog and videos of electronic experiments on my YouTube channel.

By computing, I aim the programming of ATMega microcontrollers therefore programming in C/C++. Creating applications for OS (Windows and Linux) is also part of my hobby as well as the creation of Android application in Java. I also do Web development (HTML5, PHP, CSS3, JavaScript) and for nearly two years, we (a friend and I) are building a website to present my compositions.

Compositions ? Let me explain. I’m guitarist since a decade now. I am also, in my spare time, pianist and drummer. To be honest, I like to practice any type of instrument, but what I like most is the guitar and singing. That’s why for about 6 years I compose my personal songs. I do all the instrumental part and the lyrics by myself. I will have the opportunity to present all of this in an article on my blog. To discover my creations, go to my website at www.songaccoustic.fr.

Beside these technical and musical passions, I’m a lover of nature, ecology and sustainable development. I wouldn’t classify myself as a protector of nature because I do not belong to any association acting for this purpose, but each of my actions are reflected in that way.
